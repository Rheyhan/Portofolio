This is me

I'm Rheyhan Fahry

A data scientist with a burning passion for teaching computer on how to think and act like human does. I specialize in translating complex concepts into seamless automation. I enjoy building systems that don't just work efficiently but resonate with the people using them, and I'm always looking for ways to improve both my technical skills and the user experience.

My Story

How I got here

The Beginning

I started off programming when I was 17 years old. My first language was LUA. I was so fascinated by the idea of creating helpful tools that could give me an advantage over others in game.

Over the years, I explored various programming languages and technologies, gradually deciding to focus on data science due to my study in Statistics. The ability to teach computers to learn from data and make what's seemingly intelligent over humans fascinated me.
