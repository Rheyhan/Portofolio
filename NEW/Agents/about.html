My Story

How I got here

The Beginning

I started off programming when I was 17 years old. My first language was LUA. I was so fascinated by the idea of creating helpful tools that could give me an advantage over others in game.

Over the years, I explored various programming languages and technologies, gradually deciding to focus on data science due to my study in Statistics. The ability to teach computers to learn from data and make what's seemingly intelligent decision over humans have fascinated me.

Where I Am Now

I just graduated from university in December 2025 and currently looking for a full-time position as a data scientist or anything related to machine learning and AI. In the meantime, I am working on several personal projects to sharpen my skills and expand my portfolio.

Solely focusing on data science and machine learning has allowed me to dive deep into the field, vouching knowledge and hands-on experience in building computer assisted tools. However, I am always open to explore other fields in software development as well.

Looking Forward

I aim to continuously grow as a data scientist and machine learning engineer, staying updated with the latest advancements in AI and related technologies.

The current attention I'm staring at as of now is on large language models and generative AI. I believe these technologies have the potential to revolutionize how we interact with computers and access new information.
